## Notes on Personalization

- Reference their specific materials (steel, aluminum, titanium) if visible on their site
- Reference their location — local pride matters with shop owners
- If they have a public on-time delivery claim, reference it ("you mention 98% on-time — I'd love to see how your sequencing achieves that")
- Avoid jargon: say "what runs next on your machines" not "production scheduling algorithm"
