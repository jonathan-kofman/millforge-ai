## Search Sources

- **Thomasnet.com** — search "CNC machining" + state. Operators have direct contacts.
- **LinkedIn** — search "job shop" + "operations manager" or "owner" + location
- **MFG.com** — marketplace where shops list capacity; direct contact info often visible
- **Local manufacturing associations** — NTMA chapters (National Tooling & Machining Association)
- **Manufacturer's reps** — they know every shop in a region

## Qualifying Questions (for cold outreach)

1. How many active orders are you running at once?
2. How do you currently schedule production — spreadsheet, ERP, whiteboard?
3. What's your on-time delivery rate?
4. How often do you have to shuffle jobs because of a rush order?

Any "spreadsheet" or "whiteboard" answer = strong lead.

## What We're Looking For

- Willing to share 30–90 days of order history (material, quantity, due date, machine)
- Unhappy with current scheduling process
- 10–200 employees (small enough to move fast, large enough to feel the pain)
- Not already running Plex/Epicor/JobBOSS with full utilization
